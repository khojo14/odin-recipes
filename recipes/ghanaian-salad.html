Ghanaian Salad

Description

This a Ghanaian salad, made of vegetabes, some protein and a salad cream or mayonnaise dressing.

It is usually served as an accompaniment or taken with bread.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Green Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Boiled Eggs
  • Canned Sardine
  • Baked Beans
  • Salad Cream or Mayonnaise
  • Kecthup

Steps

Please ensure all vegetables are washed thoroughly.

  1. Cut or tear the lettuce into small pieces and set aside in a bowl.
  2. Cut onions to preferable sizes and add to the bowl.
  3. Cut green pepper to preferable sizes and add to the bowl.
  4. Grate carrot into fine parts and add to the bowl.
  5. Add a little salad cream (or mayonnaise) and mix everything.
  6. Cut eggs into circular parts and spread evenly on top of the mixture.
  7. Cut cucumber into thin circular parts and spread evenly on top of the mixture.
  8. Cut sardines into chunks and spread evenly on top of the mixture.
  9. Spread baked beans evenly on top of the mixture.
  10. Spread salad cream (or mayonnaise) evenly on top of the mixture.
  11. Add a little spread of ketchup.

Your salad is ready!
